## Notes:

Ver 1.0 - Features 

**DARKY Ver.**

            MSX Internal PSG - 3Chs as BGM (mono 3.579545Mhz) + 
            DARKY PSG-A - 3Chs as BGM (stereo 3.6Mhz) +
            DARKY PSG-B - 1Chs as Sound Effect 
            7 channels in total

**MegaFlashROM SCC+ SD Ver.**

            MSX Internal PSG - 3Chs as BGM (mono 3.579545Mhz) +
            MegaFlashROM SCC+ SD Ext PSG - 1Chs as Sound Effect +
            4 channels in total
